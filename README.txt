tic tac toe
Two players game
One players will play with X and the other player will play with O.
Only one player can play at a time (in turns)
The players that successfully places 3 marks (X or O) in a horizontal, verticle or diagonal riw will win the game
The final winner of the game will be displayed on the screen



Scrabble 
Multi player game
The system should generate a random word
Player 1 will be displayed with a word that has been generated randomly
Player 1 is required to draw and player 2 will guess the word
The points will be awarded on the time taken for the player to guess the word
The points will be displayed on the screen
